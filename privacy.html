Privacy Policy — Total Fishing NZ

Effective date: 11 July 2026
App: Total Fishing NZ (iOS)
Developer: Anna‑Maree Morris, trading as NZ Heritage Nature, Bannockburn, Central Otago, New Zealand
Contact: annamareemorris@icloud.com

Total Fishing NZ is built to respect your privacy. In plain terms: the app does not collect, store, or share your personal information on our servers. We have no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking.

What stays on your device

  • Your catch log and photos. Anything you log — catches, notes, and photos — is stored only on your device (and in your own iCloud backup if you have that enabled). We never receive it, and you can delete any entry at any time.
  • Your saved spots and settings. Favourite spots and app preferences are stored on your device only.

Location

The app uses your location only while you are using it, and only on your device, to show nearby access points, centre the map, and give walking/driving directions. Your location is not collected, stored, or transmitted to us, and it is not shared with any third party by us. Directions use Apple Maps' routing on your device. You can decline or revoke location access in iOS Settings at any time; the rest of the app still works.

Purchases

Subscriptions are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not see or receive your name, payment card, or billing details — Apple only tells the app whether a subscription is active.

Information the app fetches (and what it sends)

To show live conditions, the app requests data from public sources using fixed river locations (not your personal location):

  • Environment Southland — river flow, water temperature, and webcam imagery.
  • Apple WeatherKit — weather; governed by Apple's privacy terms.
  • Open‑Meteo — a short weather forecast.
  • Fish and Game Southland — angler access information (bundled in the app).
  • A small configuration file we host, used to enable or disable partner data at a partner's request.

These requests fetch public information. They do not send us your identity, your location, or your catch log.

Tracking and advertising

There is none. The app does not track you across other apps or websites, contains no advertising, and uses no third‑party analytics or tracking tools.

Children

The app is a general‑audience fishing tool and is not directed at children. It does not knowingly collect information from anyone.

Data retention and deletion

Because your content lives on your device, you control it: delete individual entries in the app, or remove the app to erase its data from the device.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the effective date above.

Contact
